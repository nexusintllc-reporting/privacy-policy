# Privacy Policy for Daily Azan - Prayer Times, Quran & Islamic Content

**Last updated: June 14, 2026**

## Introduction

This privacy policy applies to the Daily Azan App (the "App"). This App is provided by Malik Lakhani ("I", "me", or "my").

The App provides: Prayer Times (Azan), Quran reading & listening, Daily Hadith, Daily Durood, Daily Dua, and 99 Names of Allah with notifications.

## Data Collection

**This App does NOT collect, store, or share any personal data with third parties.**

- No account creation or login required
- No tracking cookies or analytics tools
- No personal information is ever transmitted to any server
- All data remains solely on your device

## Permissions Required

### Location Permission
- Used to calculate accurate prayer times based on your current GPS position
- Location is accessed ONLY when the app is open or when background prayer time calculation runs
- Location data is NEVER stored, saved, or transmitted anywhere
- You can manually select your city instead of using GPS

### Notifications Permission
- Used to send daily reminders for prayer times, Quran verses, Hadith, Durood, Dua, and 99 Names of Allah
- All notifications are generated locally on your device
- You can customize or disable any notification in app settings

### Background Execution
- The app needs to run brief background tasks to check prayer times and deliver notifications
- No data is collected or transmitted during background execution
- For best experience, please allow the app to ignore battery optimization

### Storage Permission
- Used to cache Quran audio files for offline playback
- Used to save your app preferences, bookmarks, and settings
- All stored data remains on your device only

### Internet Access
- Required only to download Quran audio, translations, and prayer time data when needed
- No personal data is ever transmitted over the internet

## Data Storage

**All data is stored locally on your device only:**

| Data Type | Location | Deletion Method |
|-----------|----------|-----------------|
| Prayer time settings | Local storage | Clear app data or uninstall |
| Bookmarked ayahs | Local storage | Remove bookmark or clear data |
| Notification preferences | Local storage | Change in settings or clear data |
| Cached audio files | Local cache | Clear app cache |
| App settings | Local storage | Reset settings or clear data |

## How to Delete Your Data

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you can delete it by:

1. **Uninstalling the app** - This removes all app data from your device
2. **Clearing app data** - Go to Android Settings → Apps → Daily Azan → Storage → Clear Data
3. **Manually removing bookmarks** - Delete individual bookmarks within the app

No action is needed on our part as we do not store any of your data on external servers.

## Third-Party Services

The App uses these services for functionality only (no data is sent to them):

| Service | Purpose | Data Sent |
|---------|---------|-----------|
| Aladhan API | Prayer time calculation | None (only requests public prayer times) |
| Quran.com API | Quran text and translations | None (only requests public Quran data) |
| Quran Audio APIs | Audio recitation streaming | None (only requests audio files) |

**Important:** These services receive ONLY public API requests (like "give me prayer times for Mecca"). They receive NO personal information about you.

## Children's Privacy

This App does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13. The App is safe for all ages as it requires no personal data.
